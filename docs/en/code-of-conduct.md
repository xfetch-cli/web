# Code of Conduct

Xfetch contributors and users are expected to collaborate in a respectful, inclusive, and professional way.

## Our Standards

- Communicate with respect and technical clarity
- Provide constructive feedback on code, docs, and design decisions
- Welcome contributors across platforms and experience levels
- Keep discussions focused on improving xfetch for everyone

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks, mockery, or hostile escalation
- Deliberate disruption of project discussions or reviews
- Publishing private information without permission

## Reporting

Report incidents to [x@xscriptor.com](mailto:x@xscriptor.com). Reports are treated confidentially.

**Thank you for helping maintain a healthy open-source community.**
